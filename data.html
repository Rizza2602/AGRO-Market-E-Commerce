Methodology

Evaluation Instruments & Platforms

We utilized a mix of industry-standard evaluation frameworks and digital communication tools to gather comprehensive user data.

Heuristic Evaluation

A systematic inspection of the user interface based on 10 Usability Heuristics (Nielsen) to identify potential UX friction points.

Google Forms

Primary tool for quantitative data collection. We deployed structured surveys to measure the System Usability Scale (SUS) score.

Google Meet

Used for moderated remote usability testing sessions, allowing us to observe user behavior via screen sharing and interviews.

Usability Testing

Task-based scenarios performed by participants to evaluate the effectiveness and efficiency of the Agro Market workflow.

Participants

User Profiles

  • Farmers: Primary users testing the selling modules.
  • Buyers: Evaluated the checkout and product discovery flow.
  • IT Students: Performed technical usability audits.
  • Evaluators: Conducted the final heuristic analysis.
