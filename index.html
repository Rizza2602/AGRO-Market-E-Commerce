Project Overview

Agro Market E-Commerce System

Our system is a dedicated web-based agricultural marketplace designed to bridge the gap between local farmers and urban buyers. By providing a direct-to-consumer platform, we eliminate unnecessary middlemen, ensuring better profit margins for producers and fresher products for the community.
